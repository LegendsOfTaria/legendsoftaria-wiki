# Firemaking

**Firemaking** lets players burn logs at a campfire to produce <item:coal> and gain Firemaking experience.

## Getting Started

To train Firemaking, interact with a campfire tile and choose **Burn Wood**. You must have the required logs in your inventory and meet the level requirement.

## Firemaking Recipes

All current recipes are available through **Burn Wood** at a campfire.

| Recipe | Level | Input | Output | XP | Time |
|--------|-------|-------|--------|----|------|
| Burn <item:birch-logs> | 1 | 1x <item:birch-logs> | 1x <item:coal> | 9 | 5s |
| Burn <item:oak-logs> | 15 | 1x <item:oak-logs> | 1x <item:coal> | 26 | 5.5s |
| Burn <item:pine-logs> | 30 | 1x <item:pine-logs> | 1x <item:coal> | 44 | 6s |

## Training Tips

1. **Train with Woodcutting**: Chop your own logs, then burn them for steady progression.
2. **Use your highest unlocked log**: Higher-tier logs give better Firemaking XP.
3. **Batch burning helps**: Bring full inventories of logs to reduce downtime.
4. **Levels 1-15**: Burn <item:birch-logs> for early gains.
5. **Levels 15-30**: Switch to <item:oak-logs> once available.
6. **Levels 30-45**: <item:pine-logs> provide solid mid-level XP.
7. **Levels 45-55**: <item:cedar-logs> for continued gains.
8. **Levels 55-65**: <item:maple-logs> unlock better rates.
9. **Levels 65-75**: <item:linden-logs> push into the upper tiers.
10. **Level 75+**: <item:cherry-logs> give the best XP rates.
