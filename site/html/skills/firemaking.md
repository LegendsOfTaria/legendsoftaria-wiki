# Firemaking

**Firemaking** lets players burn logs at a campfire to produce <item:coal> and gain Firemaking experience.

## Getting Started

To train Firemaking, interact with a campfire tile and choose **Burn Wood**. You must have the required logs in your inventory and meet the level requirement.

## Firemaking Recipes

All current recipes are available through **Burn Wood** at a campfire.

| Recipe | Level | Input | Output | XP | Time |
|--------|-------|-------|--------|----|------|
| Burn <item:birch-logs> | 1 | 1x <item:birch-logs> | 1x <item:coal> | 10 | 3.0s |
| Burn <item:oak-logs> | 10 | 1x <item:oak-logs> | 1x <item:coal> | 30 | 3.0s |
| Burn <item:pine-logs> | 20 | 1x <item:pine-logs> | 1x <item:coal> | 50 | 3.0s |
| Burn <item:cedar-logs> | 30 | 1x <item:cedar-logs> | 1x <item:coal> | 70 | 3.0s |

## Training Tips

1. **Train with Woodcutting**: Chop your own logs, then burn them for steady progression.
2. **Use your highest unlocked log**: Higher-tier logs give better Firemaking XP.
3. **Batch burning helps**: Bring full inventories of logs to reduce downtime.
