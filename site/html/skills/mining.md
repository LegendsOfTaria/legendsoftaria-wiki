# ![Mining](/assets/images/skills/mining.png) Mining

**Mining** is a gathering skill that allows players to extract ores from rocks scattered throughout the world. Ores can be smelted into bars at a furnace, which are then used in [Smithing](/skills/smithing.html) to create weapons and armor.

## Getting Started

To begin mining, you'll need a pickaxe equipped in your hotbar. Approach a rock and tap on it to start mining. Your success chance depends on the rock type, your Mining level, and the quality of your pickaxe.

## Pickaxes

| Pickaxe | Level | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| <item:bronze-pickaxe> | 1 | Starting pickaxe, can be smithed or bought |
| <item:iron-pickaxe> | 10 | Required for <item:mithril-ore> |
| <item:mithril-pickaxe> | 20 | Required for <item:adamant-ore> |
| <item:adamant-pickaxe> | 30 | Best pickaxe available |

## Ores

| Ore | Level | XP | Respawn Time | Notes |
|-----|-------|-----|--------------|-------|
| <item:copper-ore> | 1 | 10 | 4-6 seconds | Used with <item:tin-ore> to make <item:bronze-bar> |
| <item:tin-ore> | 1 | 10 | 4-6 seconds | Used with <item:copper-ore> to make <item:bronze-bar> |
| <item:iron-ore> | 10 | 20 | 5-8 seconds | Smelted into <item:iron-bar> |
| <item:mithril-ore> | 20 | 40 | 7-10 seconds | Requires <item:iron-pickaxe> or better |
| <item:adamant-ore> | 30 | 80 | 9-12 seconds | Requires <item:mithril-pickaxe> or better |

## Training Tips

1. **Levels 1-10**: Mine <item:copper-ore> and <item:tin-ore> ore. These are abundant in the starting areas and respawn quickly.
2. **Levels 10-20**: Switch to <item:iron-ore> ore for better XP. Iron rocks are slightly slower but give double the experience.
3. **Levels 20-30**: Mine <item:mithril-ore> ore. Make sure you have at least an <item:iron-pickaxe>.
4. **Levels 30+**: <item:adamant-ore> ore provides the best XP but requires a <item:mithril-pickaxe>.

## Mining Locations

- **Starting Area**: <item:copper-ore> and <item:tin-ore> rocks near the spawn point
- **Iron Mine**: Located east of the main town
- **Mithril Caverns**: Found in the mountain region
- **Adamant Depths**: Deep underground, accessed through the mining guild
