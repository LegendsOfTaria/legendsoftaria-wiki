# ![Mining](/assets/images/skills/mining.png) Mining

**Mining** is a gathering skill that allows players to extract ores from rocks scattered throughout the world. Ores can be smelted into bars at a furnace, which are then used in [Smithing](/skills/smithing.html) to create weapons and armor.

## Getting Started

To begin mining, you'll need a pickaxe equipped in your hotbar. Approach a rock and tap on it to start mining. Your success chance depends on the rock type, your Mining level, and the quality of your pickaxe.

## Training Tips

1. **Levels 1-15**: Mine <item:copper-ore> and <item:tin-ore>. These are abundant in the starting areas and respawn quickly.
2. **Levels 15-30**: Switch to <item:iron-ore> for better XP. No special pickaxe required.
3. **Levels 30-45**: Mine <item:mithril-ore>. Make sure you have at least an <item:iron-pickaxe>.
4. **Levels 45-60**: <item:adamant-ore> gives solid XP. Requires a <item:mithril-pickaxe>.
5. **Levels 60-75**: <item:viridium-ore> provides excellent XP. Requires an <item:adamant-pickaxe>.
6. **Level 75+**: <item:emberite-ore> gives the best XP. Requires a <item:viridium-pickaxe>.

## Mining Locations

- **Starting Area**: <item:copper-ore> and <item:tin-ore> rocks near the spawn point
- **Iron Mine**: Located east of the main town
- **Mithril Caverns**: Found in the mountain region
- **Adamant Depths**: Deep underground, accessed through the mining guild
- **Viridium Quarry**: High-level zone requiring level 60 Mining
- **Emberite Depths**: End-game zone requiring level 75 Mining
