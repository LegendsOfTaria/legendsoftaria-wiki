# Woodcutting

**Woodcutting** is a gathering skill that lets players chop trees into logs. Logs are used in [Firemaking](/skills/firemaking.html) and future crafting content.

## Getting Started

To begin woodcutting, equip an axe in your hotbar. Approach a tree and tap **Chop**. Your success chance depends on the tree type and your current axe.

## Training Tips

1. **Levels 1-10**: Chop [![Birch Logs](/assets/images/items/birch-logs.png) Birch](/items/birch-logs.html) trees for quick gains.
2. **Levels 10-20**: Move to [![Oak Logs](/assets/images/items/oak-logs.png) Oak](/items/oak-logs.html) once you have an [![Iron Axe](/assets/images/items/iron-axe.png) Iron Axe](/items/iron-axe.html).
3. **Levels 20-30**: Focus [![Pine Logs](/assets/images/items/pine-logs.png) Pine](/items/pine-logs.html) with a [![Mithril Axe](/assets/images/items/mithril-axe.png) Mithril Axe](/items/mithril-axe.html).
4. **Levels 30+**: Chop [![Cedar Logs](/assets/images/items/cedar-logs.png) Cedar](/items/cedar-logs.html) for the highest current XP.
