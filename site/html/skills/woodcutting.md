# Woodcutting

**Woodcutting** is a gathering skill that lets players chop trees into logs. Logs are used in [Firemaking](/skills/firemaking.html) and future crafting content.

## Getting Started

To begin woodcutting, equip an axe in your hotbar. Approach a tree and tap **Chop**. Your success chance depends on the tree type and your current axe.

## Axes

| Axe | Level | Notes |
|-----|-------|-------|
| <item:bronze-axe> | 1 | Starting axe for early trees |
| <item:iron-axe> | 10 | Required minimum for Oak trees |
| <item:mithril-axe> | 20 | Required minimum for Pine trees |
| <item:adamant-axe> | 30 | Required minimum for Cedar trees |

## Trees

| Tree | Level | XP | Respawn Time | Minimum Axe | Notes |
|------|-------|----|--------------|-------------|-------|
| <item:birch-logs> | 1 | 10 | 4-6 seconds | <item:bronze-axe> | Fastest early training |
| <item:oak-logs> | 10 | 20 | 5-8 seconds | <item:iron-axe> | Better XP than Birch |
| <item:pine-logs> | 20 | 40 | 7-10 seconds | <item:mithril-axe> | Strong mid-level XP |
| <item:cedar-logs> | 30 | 80 | 9-12 seconds | <item:adamant-axe> | Best currently released tree |

## Training Tips

1. **Levels 1-10**: Chop <item:birch-logs> trees for quick gains.
2. **Levels 10-20**: Move to <item:oak-logs> once you have an <item:iron-axe>.
3. **Levels 20-30**: Focus <item:pine-logs> with a <item:mithril-axe>.
4. **Levels 30+**: Chop <item:cedar-logs> for the highest current XP.
