# Woodcutting

**Woodcutting** is a gathering skill that lets players chop trees into logs. Logs are used in [Firemaking](/skills/firemaking.html) and future crafting content.

## Getting Started

To begin woodcutting, equip an axe in your hotbar. Approach a tree and tap **Chop**. Your success chance depends on the tree type and your current axe.

## Axes

| Axe | Level | Notes |
|-----|-------|-------|
| <item:bronze-axe> | 1 | Starting axe for Birch trees |
| <item:iron-axe> | 15 | Required minimum for Oak trees |
| <item:mithril-axe> | 30 | Required minimum for Pine trees |
| <item:adamant-axe> | 45 | Required minimum for Cedar and Maple trees |
| <item:viridium-axe> | 60 | Required minimum for Linden trees |
| <item:emberite-axe> | 75 | Required minimum for Cherry trees |

## Trees

| Tree | Level | XP | Respawn Time | Minimum Axe | Notes |
|------|-------|----|--------------|-------------|-------|
| <item:birch-logs> | 1 | 9 | 4-6 seconds | <item:bronze-axe> | Fastest early training |
| <item:oak-logs> | 15 | 26 | 5-8 seconds | <item:iron-axe> | Better XP than Birch |
| <item:pine-logs> | 30 | 44 | 7-10 seconds | <item:mithril-axe> | Strong mid-level XP |
| <item:cedar-logs> | 45 | 66 | 9-12 seconds | <item:adamant-axe> | Good upper-mid tree |
| <item:maple-logs> | 55 | 88 | 11-15 seconds | <item:adamant-axe> | High-level gains |
| <item:linden-logs> | 65 | 114 | 13-17 seconds | <item:viridium-axe> | Near end-game tree |
| <item:cherry-logs> | 75 | 141 | 15-20 seconds | <item:emberite-axe> | Best XP available |

## Training Tips

1. **Levels 1-15**: Chop <item:birch-logs> trees for quick gains.
2. **Levels 15-30**: Move to <item:oak-logs> once you have an <item:iron-axe>.
3. **Levels 30-45**: Focus <item:pine-logs> with a <item:mithril-axe>.
4. **Levels 45-55**: Chop <item:cedar-logs> with an <item:adamant-axe>.
5. **Levels 55-65**: <item:maple-logs> trees (also requires <item:adamant-axe>) give better rates.
6. **Levels 65-75**: Switch to <item:linden-logs> once you have a <item:viridium-axe>.
7. **Level 75+**: <item:cherry-logs> provide the highest XP, requiring an <item:emberite-axe>.
