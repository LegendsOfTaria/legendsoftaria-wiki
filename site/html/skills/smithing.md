# ![Smithing](/assets/images/skills/smithing.png) Smithing

**Smithing** is a production skill that allows players to smelt ores into bars at a furnace and forge those bars into weapons and armor at an anvil.

## Getting Started

Smithing has two components:
1. **Smelting**: Use a furnace to convert ores into metal bars
2. **Forging**: Use an anvil to craft bars into equipment

## Smelting

Use a furnace to smelt ores into bars. Each bar type requires a minimum Smithing level.

| Bar | Level | Materials | XP | Time |
|-----|-------|-----------|-----|------|
| <item:bronze-bar> | 1 | <item:copper-ore> + <item:tin-ore> | 2 | 3 seconds |
| <item:iron-bar> | 10 | <item:iron-ore> | 4 | 3 seconds |
| <item:mithril-bar> | 20 | <item:mithril-ore> | 8 | 3 seconds |
| <item:adamant-bar> | 30 | <item:adamant-ore> | 16 | 3 seconds |

## Training Tips

1. **Levels 1-10**: Smelt <item:bronze-bar> and smith Bronze equipment. Focus on swords first as they only need 1 bar.
2. **Levels 10-20**: Switch to <item:iron-bar> smelting and smithing. The XP doubles compared to Bronze.
3. **Levels 20-30**: Work with <item:mithril-bar> for better XP rates.
4. **Levels 30+**: <item:adamant-bar> provides the best XP but requires significant material investment.

## Time Efficiency

- **Smelting**: 3 seconds per bar
- **Smithing**: 5 seconds per item

All smithing operations can be queued - select the item to craft, choose a quantity, and your character will continue until materials run out or you cancel.
