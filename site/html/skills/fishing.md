# ![Fishing](/assets/images/skills/fishing.png) Fishing

**Fishing** is a gathering skill that allows players to catch fish from various fishing spots. Fish can be cooked and eaten to restore health.

## Getting Started

To fish, you need the appropriate fishing equipment in your hotbar and bait (for most methods). Approach a fishing spot and tap on it to start fishing. Different spots require different tools and levels.

## Fishing Equipment

| Tool | Level | Notes |

### Harpoon Fishing (Level 67+)

| Tool | Level | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|

## Bait

All fishing methods require <item:fishing-bait>. Make sure to stock up before heading to fishing spots!

## Training Tips

1. **Levels 1-22**: Use a <item:fishing-net> to catch <item:raw-shrimp>, <item:raw-anchovies>, and <item:raw-sardine>.
2. **Levels 22-45**: Switch to Rod fishing for <item:raw-trout>, <item:raw-bass>, and <item:raw-salmon>.
3. **Levels 45-67**: Use <item:cage> to catch <item:raw-crab>, <item:raw-lobster>, and <item:raw-octopus>.
4. **Levels 67+**: Harpoon fishing for the best XP rates with <item:raw-swordfish>, <item:raw-shark>, <item:raw-manta-ray>, and <item:raw-tuna>.

## Success Rates

Success chance depends on:
- Your Fishing level
- The tool you're using (better tools have higher success bonuses)
- The type of fish (harder fish have lower base rates)

| Method | Base Success Rate |
|--------|------------------|
| Net | 60% |
| Rod | 55% |
| Cage | 50% |
| Harpoon | 40% |

Better tools add a success bonus on top of the base rate.
